About the Project

For this concept project, I wanted to create a dream restaurant that incorporated support for the local community & farmers while focusing on sustainable food growth & raising awareness of where food comes from. The ever-changing menu focuses on in-season produce & features items with a vegetarian focus but also contains meat & vegan options. I used straight HTML & CSS to create a not-quite-minimalist site with a fresh & bright color palette. Using vegetable & farm images further emphasized the focus on sustainability with smaller farming practices.
