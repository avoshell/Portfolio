ABOUT THE PROJECT

I worked on a team for the 2017 DSM Hack to create a virtual brain for On With Life, a local non-profit that focuses on brain injury rehabilitation. They wanted a brain tool to use for demonstrating the location and function of each section of the brain.

My role within the team was working on the front-end design and functionality. I was responsible for designing and executing the overall page UX, focusing mostly on layout and navigation of the tool.

View the live project at the On With Life page.
